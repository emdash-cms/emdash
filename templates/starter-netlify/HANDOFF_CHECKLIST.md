# Client Handoff Checklist

## Access

- [ ] Client has admin login and recovery method verified.
- [ ] Hosting account access confirmed (Vercel/Netlify dashboard).
- [ ] Domain and DNS ownership confirmed.

## Content

- [ ] Business name/tagline/URL set.
- [ ] Contact details and hours filled.
- [ ] Contact form tested end-to-end.
- [ ] Logo/favicon uploaded and visible.
- [ ] Home, About, Contact, and at least one post published.

## Quality

- [ ] `pnpm check:images` passes.
- [ ] `pnpm check:seo` passes.
- [ ] Mobile spot-check complete.
- [ ] 404 page reviewed.

## Operations

- [ ] Backup plan documented for DB and media.
- [ ] Update cadence agreed (monthly/quarterly).
- [ ] Support contact and SLA documented.
