Redesigns the content editor layout. The editor now fills the viewport with a distraction-lighter writing column, and all publish controls live in a structural settings panel on the end side: Save, Publish, Preview, autosave status, and publish-state badges sit in an action bar pinned above the panel's sections (slug, scheduling, ownership, bylines, translations, taxonomies, SEO, outline, revisions), with Move to Trash isolated at the bottom. Below the `lg` breakpoint the panel becomes a slide-in sheet behind a Settings button, while Save and autosave feedback stay visible in the editor header. The layout mirrors correctly in RTL locales.
